# URP-IP29: Relationship Leverage Analysis

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Understanding leverage and dependency in relationships
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any relationship context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Dependency Mapping**: What does each party need from the other?
2. **Alternative Assessment**: What alternatives does each party have?
3. **Leverage Calculation**: Who has leverage? How much?
4. **Leverage Dynamics**: How might leverage shift over time?
5. **Strategy Adjustment**: How does leverage inform strategy?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Leverage comes from alternatives. The party who needs the relationship less has more power. Know your leverage—and theirs."
