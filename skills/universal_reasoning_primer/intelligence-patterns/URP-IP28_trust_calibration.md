# URP-IP28: Trust Calibration

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Calibrating appropriate trust levels
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any relationship context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Track Record Assessment**: What's the party's history?
2. **Incentive Analysis**: What incentives does the party have?
3. **Stakes Calibration**: What's at risk with different trust levels?
4. **Trust Level Setting**: What trust level is appropriate?
5. **Trust Verification**: How do I verify trust is warranted?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Trust but verify isn't cynicism—it's calibration. Trust appropriately based on evidence, incentives, and stakes."
