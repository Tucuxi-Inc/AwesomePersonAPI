# URP-IP23: Multi-Vector Recovery Coordination

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Coordinating simultaneous recovery efforts across domains
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any recovery context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Vector Identification**: What domains/workstreams need recovery?
2. **Dependency Mapping**: How do recovery vectors depend on each other?
3. **Sequencing**: What sequence optimizes overall recovery?
4. **Resource Coordination**: How are resources shared across vectors?
5. **Progress Synchronization**: How do I keep vectors aligned?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Recovery isn't a single thread—it's multiple parallel workstreams that must stay synchronized. Coordinate across vectors or watch them diverge."
