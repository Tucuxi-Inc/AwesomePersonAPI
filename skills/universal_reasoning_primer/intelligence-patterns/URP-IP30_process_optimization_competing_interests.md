# URP-IP30: Process Optimization Under Competing Interests

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Optimizing processes when stakeholders have competing interests
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any process design context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Interest Identification**: What does each stakeholder want from the process?
2. **Conflict Mapping**: Where do interests conflict?
3. **Tradeoff Analysis**: What tradeoffs are inherent?
4. **Optimization Design**: How do I optimize for overall value?
5. **Stakeholder Alignment**: How do I get buy-in for the optimized process?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Process design is political. When stakeholders have different interests, the process that emerges reflects a negotiation, not just optimization."
