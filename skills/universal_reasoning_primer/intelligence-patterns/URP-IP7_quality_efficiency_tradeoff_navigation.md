# URP-IP7: Quality-Efficiency Tradeoff Navigation

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Managing quality-efficiency tensions
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any production/delivery context
**Orchestration Role:** Enables appropriate tradeoff decisions under constraints

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Tradeoff Recognition**: Where do quality and efficiency conflict?
2. **Constraint Identification**: What constraints drive the tradeoff?
3. **Value Analysis**: What's the value of quality vs. efficiency in this context?
4. **Optimal Point Selection**: Where's the right balance for this situation?
5. **Communication**: How do I explain the tradeoff to stakeholders?

### Expert Heuristic

> "There's a time for 'good enough fast' and a time for 'excellent slow.' Know which situation you're in."

## Evidence from Literature

- **Pareto Efficiency**: Tradeoff frontiers
- **Quality Management**: Acceptable quality levels
- **Satisficing**: Good enough vs. optimal
- **Time-Cost-Quality**: Project management triangle
