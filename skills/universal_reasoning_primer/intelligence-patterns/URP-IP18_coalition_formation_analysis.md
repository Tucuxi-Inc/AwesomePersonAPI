# URP-IP18: Coalition Formation Analysis

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Analyzing potential alliances and coalition dynamics
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any multi-party political context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Interest Mapping**: What are each party's interests?
2. **Alignment Analysis**: Which parties have aligned interests?
3. **Coalition Viability**: What coalitions could form? How stable?
4. **Blocking Coalition Risk**: What coalitions could block my objectives?
5. **Coalition Strategy**: What coalition should I pursue/enable?

### Expert Heuristic

> "In multi-party dynamics, the winning move is often not convincing everyone, but building the right coalition. Map who could align with whom."
