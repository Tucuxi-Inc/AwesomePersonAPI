# URP-IP19: Crisis Intelligence Synthesis

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Rapid information synthesis under crisis conditions
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any crisis context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Source Triage**: What information sources are available? Which are reliable?
2. **Rapid Synthesis**: Quickly integrate information into coherent picture
3. **Gap Identification**: What critical information is missing?
4. **Situational Assessment**: What's the current state? How is it evolving?
5. **Update Cycle**: How do I continuously update as new information arrives?

### Expert Heuristic

> "In crisis, speed of understanding matters as much as depth. Get to 'good enough' situational awareness fast; refine as you go."

## Evidence from Literature

- **Crisis Management**: Rapid sensemaking under pressure
- **Military Intelligence**: Time-critical synthesis
- **Emergency Response**: Real-time situational awareness
- **Incident Command**: Information flow in crisis
