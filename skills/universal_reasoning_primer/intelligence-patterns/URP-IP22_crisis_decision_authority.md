# URP-IP22: Crisis Decision Authority

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Establishing decision authority under crisis conditions
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any crisis context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Authority Assessment**: What authority structure applies in this crisis?
2. **Gap Identification**: Where is normal authority insufficient?
3. **Emergency Authority**: What emergency decision rights are activated?
4. **Communication**: Ensure all parties know who decides what
5. **Documentation**: Record decisions and authority basis

### Expert Heuristic

> "Crisis demands faster decisions than normal governance allows. Establish who decides what before you need to decide anything."
