# URP-IP20: Crisis Communication Sequencing

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Sequencing stakeholder communications during crisis
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any crisis context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Stakeholder Prioritization**: Who needs to know what, in what order?
2. **Sequence Design**: What's the optimal communication sequence?
3. **Message Calibration**: How does message vary by stakeholder?
4. **Timing Coordination**: How do I synchronize across stakeholders?
5. **Feedback Integration**: How do I adapt based on stakeholder responses?

### Expert Heuristic

> "In crisis, who you tell first and what you tell them shapes everything that follows. Sequence strategically—don't just broadcast."
