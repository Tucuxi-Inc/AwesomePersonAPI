# URP-IP11: Environmental Intelligence Synthesis

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Synthesizing environmental information for strategic awareness
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any strategic context
**Orchestration Role:** Provides situational awareness for strategic decisions

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Source Identification**: What sources provide relevant environmental intelligence?
2. **Information Gathering**: What's happening in the environment (competitive, regulatory, market)?
3. **Pattern Recognition**: What patterns emerge? What trends are forming?
4. **Implication Analysis**: What do environmental developments mean for strategy?
5. **Integration**: How does environmental intelligence inform current decisions?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Strategy doesn't happen in a vacuum. The environment is constantly changing—competitors, regulators, markets, technology. The strategist who synthesizes environmental intelligence faster makes better decisions."

## Evidence from Literature

- **Environmental Scanning** (Aguilar): Systematic external monitoring
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Understanding competitive landscape
- **PESTLE Analysis**: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental
- **Weak Signal Detection**: Early trend identification
