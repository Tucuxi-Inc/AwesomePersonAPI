# URP-IP24: Strategic Communication Architecture

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Designing communication for strategic effect
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any communication context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Objective Definition**: What am I trying to achieve with this communication?
2. **Audience Analysis**: Who are the audiences? What do they need/want?
3. **Message Design**: What messages achieve objectives for each audience?
4. **Channel Selection**: What channels reach each audience effectively?
5. **Orchestration**: How do I coordinate communication across audiences and channels?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Communication isn't just information transfer—it's strategic action. Design communication to achieve objectives, not just to inform."
