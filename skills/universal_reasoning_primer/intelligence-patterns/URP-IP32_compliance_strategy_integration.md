# URP-IP32: Compliance-Strategy Integration

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Integrating compliance into strategy as enabler
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any regulated context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Requirement Mapping**: What compliance requirements apply?
2. **Strategic Impact Analysis**: How do requirements affect strategy?
3. **Integration Design**: How do I build compliance into strategy?
4. **Opportunity Identification**: Where does compliance create advantage?
5. **Continuous Alignment**: How do I maintain alignment as requirements evolve?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Compliance isn't just a constraint—it can be competitive advantage. Organizations that integrate compliance into strategy perform better than those who treat it as a checkbox."
