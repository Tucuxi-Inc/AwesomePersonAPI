# URP-IP27: Relationship Portfolio Management

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Managing relationships as strategic portfolio
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any relationship context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Relationship Inventory**: What relationships exist?
2. **Value Assessment**: What's the strategic value of each relationship?
3. **Investment Calibration**: How much to invest in each relationship?
4. **Portfolio Balance**: Is the portfolio appropriately diversified?
5. **Maintenance Planning**: How do I maintain relationships over time?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Not all relationships deserve equal investment. Manage your relationship portfolio strategically—invest more in high-value relationships."
