# URP-IP25: Stakeholder Influence Mapping

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Mapping influence networks among stakeholders
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any influence context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Influence Network Identification**: Who influences whom?
2. **Influence Mechanism**: How does influence flow? (Authority, trust, expertise)
3. **Key Node Identification**: Who are the influential nodes?
4. **Influence Path Design**: What path achieves influence on target decision?
5. **Execution**: Engage influence network strategically

### Expert Heuristic

> "The person who makes the decision isn't always the person you need to influence. Map the influence network—then influence the influencers."
