# URP-IP31: Standards-Based Positioning

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Using standards and benchmarks for legitimacy
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any legitimacy-seeking context

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Standard Identification**: What standards, benchmarks, or best practices apply?
2. **Positioning Assessment**: How does my position relate to standards?
3. **Legitimacy Strategy**: How do I use standards to support my position?
4. **Gap Addressing**: Where do I need to align with standards?
5. **Communication**: How do I frame position in standards-based terms?

### Expert Heuristic

> "Standards provide air cover. Positions aligned with standards are easier to defend; positions diverging from standards require stronger justification."
