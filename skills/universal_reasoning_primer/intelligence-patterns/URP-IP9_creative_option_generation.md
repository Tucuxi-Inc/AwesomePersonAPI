# URP-IP9: Creative Option Generation

**Type:** Intelligence Pattern (Tier 2)
**Function:** Generating creative alternatives beyond obvious positions
**Domain Independence:** Universal across any problem-solving context
**Orchestration Role:** Expands solution space beyond initial framing

## Pattern Definition

### Core Procedure

1. **Interest Clarification**: What are the underlying interests (not positions)?
2. **Constraint Mapping**: What constraints shape the solution space?
3. **Option Expansion**: What creative alternatives serve these interests?
4. **Integration Search**: Can multiple interests be served simultaneously?
5. **Feasibility Check**: Which creative options are actually achievable?

### Expert Heuristic

> "The first solution that comes to mind is usually not the best one. Expand the option space before narrowing. The best outcomes often come from options no one initially considered."

## Evidence from Literature

- **Interest-Based Negotiation** (Fisher & Ury): Focus on interests, not positions
- **Design Thinking**: Ideation and option generation
- **Brainstorming Research**: Creative option generation methods
- **Integrative Bargaining**: Expanding the pie
