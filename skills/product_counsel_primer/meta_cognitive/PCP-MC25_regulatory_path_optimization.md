# PCP-MC25: Regulatory Path Optimization

## Pattern Definition

### Trigger Condition
Activate when navigating regulatory requirements that offer multiple compliance paths, span jurisdictions, or where regulatory optimization creates meaningful business value.

### Core Procedure
1. **Requirement Mapping**: Catalog all applicable regulatory requirements
2. **Path Enumeration**: Identify alternative compliance paths
3. **Cross-Jurisdictional Analysis**: Map how requirements interact across jurisdictions
4. **Equivalence Discovery**: Find where compliance in one area satisfies another
5. **Threshold Identification**: Locate regulatory thresholds that change requirements
6. **Optimization Selection**: Choose path balancing compliance burden against business needs

### Expert Heuristic
"Regulations are constraints, but they're also design parameters. The expert finds paths that satisfy requirements while minimizing burden—not through evasion, but through intelligent navigation of legitimately available options."
