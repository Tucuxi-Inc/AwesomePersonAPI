# PCP-MC19: Epistemic Validation & Reconciliation

## Pattern Definition

### Trigger Condition
Activate when facing conflicting information from different sources, uncertain claims that need validation, or gaps between internal understanding and external reality.

### Core Procedure
1. **Source Mapping**: Identify all knowledge sources and their reliability profiles
2. **Conflict Detection**: Find where sources disagree or contradict
3. **Root Cause Analysis**: Determine WHY sources conflict (different data, different assumptions, different frames)
4. **Validation Strategy**: Design tests or triangulation to resolve conflicts
5. **Confidence Calibration**: Assign appropriate confidence levels to conclusions
6. **Assumption Archaeology**: Unearth hidden assumptions driving disagreements

### Expert Heuristic
"When experts disagree, the answer is rarely 'one is right.' The expert asks 'what would have to be true for each to be correct?' and often finds both are right about different aspects of a complex reality."
