# IP Ownership Clause Examples

## Work Product Assignment

### Customer-Favorable (Full Assignment)

**Example 1 - Comprehensive Assignment**
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All results and data generated from use of Customer's instruments by
or for Customer under this Agreement shall be owned exclusively by
Customer and shall be treated as Customer Confidential Information
hereunder.

Customer may use, disclose and otherwise exploit such results and data,
and products, services and intellectual property based on such results
and data, without restriction and without obligation to Vendor.
```

---

**Example 2 - Full Work Product Assignment**
```
WORK PRODUCT OWNERSHIP

All Deliverables, work product, inventions, discoveries, improvements,
developments, works of authorship, and other intellectual property
conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by Vendor in
connection with the Services ("Work Product") shall be the sole and
exclusive property of Customer.

Vendor hereby irrevocably assigns to Customer all right, title, and
interest in and to the Work Product, including all intellectual property
rights therein, throughout the world, in perpetuity.

To the extent any Work Product constitutes a "work made for hire" under
the U.S. Copyright Act, such Work Product shall be deemed a work made
for hire with Customer as the author.

Vendor shall execute all documents and take all actions reasonably
necessary to perfect Customer's ownership, including assignments,
applications, and registrations.
```

### Vendor-Favorable (License Model)

**Example 3 - Vendor Retains IP, Grants License**
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Vendor retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Software,
Services, Deliverables, and all intellectual property rights therein.
Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of any Vendor intellectual
property to Customer.

Subject to payment of all fees, Vendor grants Customer a non-exclusive,
non-transferable, limited license to use the Deliverables solely for
Customer's internal business purposes during the term.

Vendor shall retain ownership of all tools, methodologies, know-how,
techniques, and pre-existing materials used in performing the Services
("Vendor Tools"). To the extent Vendor Tools are incorporated in
Deliverables, Vendor grants Customer a perpetual, royalty-free license
to use such Vendor Tools solely as part of the Deliverables.
```

### Balanced (Joint/Split Ownership)

**Example 4 - Split Ownership Model**
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OWNERSHIP

(a) CUSTOMER IP. Customer shall own all right, title, and interest in:
    (i) Customer Data and Customer Confidential Information;
    (ii) Customer's pre-existing intellectual property;
    (iii) Deliverables specifically created for Customer under a SOW.

(b) VENDOR IP. Vendor shall own all right, title, and interest in:
    (i) Vendor's pre-existing intellectual property, tools, and methods;
    (ii) Generic or reusable components developed during the engagement;
    (iii) Improvements to Vendor's products or services.

(c) LICENSES. Vendor grants Customer a perpetual, royalty-free license
    to use Vendor IP solely as embedded in or necessary to use the
    Customer-owned Deliverables. Customer grants Vendor a perpetual,
    royalty-free license to use generic developments for other clients.
```

## Background IP

**Example 5 - Background IP Protection**
```
BACKGROUND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Each party retains all right, title, and interest in its Background IP.
"Background IP" means intellectual property owned or licensed by a party
prior to the Effective Date or developed outside the scope of this
Agreement.

Neither party acquires any rights in the other party's Background IP
except for the limited licenses expressly granted herein.

Each party grants the other a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to
use its Background IP solely to the extent necessary to perform under
or enjoy the benefits of this Agreement.
```

## Employee IP Assignment (Employment Context)

**Example 6 - Employee Invention Assignment**
```
INVENTIONS AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Employee assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in any
Inventions (ideas, discoveries, inventions, improvements, works of
authorship, software) conceived, developed, or reduced to practice
by Employee during employment, whether alone or with others, that:

(a) relate to Company's business or actual/anticipated research;
(b) result from work performed for Company; or
(c) are developed using Company resources.

Employee shall promptly disclose all Inventions and execute all
documents necessary to perfect Company's ownership. This assignment
includes all patents, copyrights, and other IP rights worldwide.

Works made within the scope of employment are "works made for hire."
To the extent not works for hire, Employee assigns all rights.

[State-specific exclusion:] This Agreement does not apply to Inventions
developed entirely on Employee's own time without Company resources
that do not relate to Company business (per [State] law).
```

## Joint Development

**Example 7 - Joint IP Ownership**
```
JOINT DEVELOPMENT

IP jointly developed by both parties under this Agreement ("Joint IP")
shall be jointly owned by the parties. Each party may use Joint IP
without the other's consent and without accounting.

Neither party may license Joint IP to third parties without the
other party's prior written consent.

If either party wishes to file for patent protection on Joint IP:
(a) It shall notify the other party and provide an opportunity to
    participate in the filing;
(b) Costs shall be shared equally unless agreed otherwise;
(c) If one party declines, the other may proceed at its expense
    and own the resulting patent.
```

## Moral Rights

**Example 8 - Moral Rights Waiver**
```
MORAL RIGHTS

To the extent permitted by applicable law, Vendor irrevocably waives
and agrees not to assert any moral rights, droit moral, or similar
rights in any Deliverables, including rights of attribution and
integrity.

Vendor acknowledges that Customer may modify, adapt, or create
derivative works from the Deliverables without notice to or consent
from Vendor.
```
