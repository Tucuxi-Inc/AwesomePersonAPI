# Governing Law and Jurisdiction Clause Examples

## Choice of Law

### Standard Choice of Law

**Example 1 - Basic Governing Law**
```
GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the State of [Delaware/New York/California], without
regard to its conflict of laws principles.
```

**Why "Without Regard to Conflicts":** Ensures the chosen state's law applies even if conflicts rules would otherwise point to another jurisdiction.

---

### Example 2 - Comprehensive Governing Law
```
GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement and any dispute arising out of or related to this
Agreement, including its formation, interpretation, performance,
breach, or termination, shall be governed by and construed in
accordance with the laws of the State of [State], without giving
effect to any choice or conflict of law provision or rule that would
cause the application of the laws of any other jurisdiction.

The United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International Sale
of Goods shall not apply to this Agreement.
```

## Jurisdiction and Venue

### Exclusive Jurisdiction

**Example 3 - Exclusive Forum Selection**
```
JURISDICTION AND VENUE

Any legal action or proceeding arising out of or relating to this
Agreement shall be brought exclusively in the federal or state courts
located in [City, County, State].

Each party irrevocably:
(a) submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of such courts;
(b) waives any objection it may have to the laying of venue in such
    courts based on inconvenient forum or any other basis;
(c) waives any objection to personal jurisdiction in such courts.

Service of process may be effected by any means permitted by applicable
law, including service by registered mail to the notice address.
```

---

### Non-Exclusive Jurisdiction

**Example 4 - Non-Exclusive Forum**
```
JURISDICTION

Each party consents to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the federal
and state courts located in [City, State] for any action arising out
of or relating to this Agreement.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, either party may:
(a) bring an action in any court of competent jurisdiction to enforce
    a judgment;
(b) seek injunctive or other equitable relief in any court of competent
    jurisdiction;
(c) bring an action in any jurisdiction where the other party has
    assets, to the extent necessary to enforce a judgment.
```

## International Considerations

**Example 5 - Cross-Border Clause**
```
GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of [England and Wales / State of Delaware].

The parties agree that any dispute arising out of or in connection
with this Agreement shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction
of the courts of [England and Wales / State of Delaware].

The United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International Sale
of Goods and the Uniform Computer Information Transactions Act (UCITA)
shall not apply to this Agreement.

Each party waives any immunity from jurisdiction or enforcement to
which it may be entitled.
```

## Federal vs. State Court

**Example 6 - Federal Court Preference**
```
VENUE

To the extent subject matter jurisdiction exists, any action arising
out of this Agreement shall be brought in the United States District
Court for the [District]. If federal subject matter jurisdiction does
not exist, venue shall be in the state courts of [County, State].

Each party waives any right to remove to federal court any action
originally filed in state court if the action does not independently
satisfy federal jurisdictional requirements.
```

## Service of Process

**Example 7 - Service Provisions**
```
SERVICE OF PROCESS

Each party agrees that service of process in any action arising out
of this Agreement may be made:

(a) by personal delivery;
(b) by overnight courier to the notice address;
(c) by registered or certified mail to the notice address;
(d) in any manner permitted by the laws of the jurisdiction where
    service is effected.

Service by mail shall be deemed complete upon deposit in the mail.

Each party appoints [Name/Agent] as its agent for service of process.
Either party may change its agent by notice to the other party.
```

---

## Judgment Enforcement

**Example 8 - Judgment Recognition**
```
ENFORCEMENT OF JUDGMENTS

Each party agrees that a final judgment in any action arising out of
this Agreement may be enforced in any jurisdiction by suit on the
judgment or in any other manner provided by law.

Each party waives any defense to enforcement based on sovereign
immunity, act of state, or other immunity from jurisdiction,
enforcement, or execution.
```

## Combined Clause

**Example 9 - Complete Governing Law Section**
```
GOVERNING LAW; JURISDICTION; VENUE

(a) GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of
    the State of [State] without regard to conflicts of law principles.

(b) JURISDICTION. Each party irrevocably submits to the exclusive
    jurisdiction of the federal and state courts located in [County,
    State] for any action arising out of or relating to this Agreement.

(c) VENUE. Each party waives any objection to venue in such courts
    based on forum non conveniens or any other basis.

(d) JURY WAIVER. EACH PARTY HEREBY WAIVES ITS RIGHT TO A JURY TRIAL
    IN ANY ACTION ARISING OUT OF THIS AGREEMENT.

(e) EXCLUSIONS. The United Nations Convention on Contracts for the
    International Sale of Goods and UCITA shall not apply.

(f) INJUNCTIVE RELIEF. Notwithstanding the foregoing, either party
    may seek injunctive or other equitable relief in any court of
    competent jurisdiction.
```
