# Warranty Clause Examples

## Overview

This skill provides real clause language examples for warranty provisions extracted from actual contracts. Examples are organized by warranty type and party position.

## Services Warranties

### Vendor-Favorable (Limited Standard)

**Example 1 - Industry Standard Warranty**
```
Vendor warrants that it will provide its services at least in accordance
with generally accepted standards prevailing in the instrument repair
industry at the time and place performed.

Warranty claims must be made within ninety (90) days after services
are performed.

VENDOR MAKES NO OTHER WARRANTIES OF ANY KIND WHATSOEVER, EXPRESS OR
IMPLIED, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO WARRANTIES OF FITNESS FOR A
PARTICULAR PURPOSE OR MERCHANTABILITY WITH RESPECT TO ITS SERVICES,
WHICH WARRANTIES ARE EXPRESSLY DISCLAIMED.

VENDOR'S SOLE LIABILITY AND RESPONSIBILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT FOR
BREACH OF WARRANTY IS RE-PERFORMANCE OF THE SERVICES WITHIN A
REASONABLE TIME OR RETURN OF THE FEE PAID FOR THE DEFECTIVE SERVICES
AT VENDOR'S OPTION. THESE ARE CUSTOMER'S SOLE AND EXCLUSIVE REMEDIES
FOR ANY BREACH OF WARRANTY.
```

**Vendor Protection Elements:**
- Low standard (industry practice, not excellence)
- Short claim window (90 days)
- Broad disclaimer of implied warranties
- Limited remedy (re-perform or refund)
- Vendor chooses the remedy
- "Sole and exclusive" language

### Balanced Position

**Example 2 - Professional Services Warranty**
```
WARRANTY

Vendor warrants that:

(a) Services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike
    manner by qualified personnel in accordance with industry
    standards and the applicable Statement of Work;

(b) Deliverables shall substantially conform to the Specifications
    set forth in the Statement of Work for a period of [90] days
    following delivery;

(c) Vendor has the right to provide the Services and grant the
    licenses contemplated herein.

REMEDY

If Services or Deliverables do not conform to the foregoing warranties,
Customer's sole remedy shall be, at Vendor's option: (a) re-performance
of the non-conforming Services; (b) repair or replacement of the
non-conforming Deliverable; or (c) refund of fees paid for the
non-conforming Services or Deliverable.

Customer must notify Vendor of any non-conformance within [30] days
of discovery and provide reasonable detail to enable Vendor to address
the issue.
```

### Customer-Favorable

**Example 3 - Comprehensive Services Warranty**
```
Vendor represents and warrants that:

(a) Services shall be performed by qualified personnel with the
    requisite skills, training, and experience, in a professional
    and workmanlike manner consistent with the highest industry
    standards;

(b) Services shall conform to the requirements and specifications
    in the applicable SOW and all mutually agreed documentation;

(c) Deliverables shall be free from material defects and shall
    perform substantially in accordance with their documentation
    for a period of [one year] from acceptance;

(d) Services shall not infringe any third-party intellectual
    property rights;

(e) Vendor shall comply with all applicable laws in performing
    the Services;

(f) No virus, malware, or disabling code shall be introduced into
    Customer's systems through the Services.

REMEDY

Customer's remedies for breach of warranty shall include, at
Customer's option: re-performance, repair, replacement, or refund,
without prejudice to any other remedies available at law or equity.
```

## Software/Product Warranties

### Vendor-Favorable

**Example 4 - Limited Software Warranty**
```
LIMITED WARRANTY

Vendor warrants that for a period of [90] days from delivery, the
Software will perform substantially in accordance with the
accompanying documentation when used on the recommended hardware
configuration.

DISCLAIMER

EXCEPT FOR THE EXPRESS WARRANTY SET FORTH ABOVE, THE SOFTWARE IS
PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND. VENDOR DISCLAIMS ALL
OTHER WARRANTIES, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED, STATUTORY OR OTHERWISE,
INCLUDING WITHOUT LIMITATION THE IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY,
FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, TITLE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

VENDOR DOES NOT WARRANT THAT THE SOFTWARE WILL BE ERROR-FREE OR
UNINTERRUPTED, OR THAT ALL DEFECTS CAN BE CORRECTED.

EXCLUSIVE REMEDY

Vendor's entire liability and Customer's exclusive remedy for any
breach of warranty shall be, at Vendor's option: (a) repair or
replacement of the Software; or (b) refund of the license fees paid.
```

### Balanced Position

**Example 5 - SaaS/Subscription Warranty**
```
SERVICE WARRANTY

Vendor warrants that during the Subscription Term:

(a) the Service will perform substantially in accordance with
    the Documentation;

(b) the functionality of the Service will not be materially
    decreased from that available at the start of the Subscription
    Term;

(c) Vendor will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the
    Service available 99.5% of the time, measured monthly,
    excluding scheduled maintenance.

DISCLAIMER

EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY PROVIDED HEREIN, VENDOR MAKES NO WARRANTIES OF
ANY KIND, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED, STATUTORY OR OTHERWISE, AND
SPECIFICALLY DISCLAIMS ALL IMPLIED WARRANTIES, INCLUDING WARRANTIES
OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND
NON-INFRINGEMENT, TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW.

REMEDY

If the Service fails to meet the warranty, Customer may: (a) request
Vendor to use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the
non-conformance; or (b) if Vendor fails to correct within [30] days,
terminate the affected Service and receive a pro-rata refund of
pre-paid fees.
```

## Comprehensive Disclaimer

### Example 6 - Full UCC Disclaimer (ALL CAPS Required)
```
DISCLAIMER OF WARRANTIES

THE WARRANTIES SET FORTH IN THIS SECTION ARE THE SOLE AND EXCLUSIVE
WARRANTIES GIVEN BY VENDOR. VENDOR HEREBY DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER
WARRANTIES, EXPRESS, IMPLIED OR STATUTORY, INCLUDING WITHOUT
LIMITATION:

(A) THE IMPLIED WARRANTY OF MERCHANTABILITY;

(B) THE IMPLIED WARRANTY OF FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE;

(C) THE IMPLIED WARRANTY OF TITLE;

(D) THE IMPLIED WARRANTY OF NON-INFRINGEMENT;

(E) ANY WARRANTY ARISING FROM COURSE OF DEALING, COURSE OF
    PERFORMANCE, OR USAGE OF TRADE.

NO ORAL OR WRITTEN INFORMATION OR ADVICE GIVEN BY VENDOR, ITS
AGENTS, OR EMPLOYEES SHALL CREATE A WARRANTY OR IN ANY WAY INCREASE
THE SCOPE OF THE WARRANTIES EXPRESSLY PROVIDED HEREIN.

SOME JURISDICTIONS DO NOT ALLOW THE EXCLUSION OF IMPLIED WARRANTIES,
SO THE ABOVE EXCLUSION MAY NOT APPLY TO CUSTOMER. IN THAT EVENT,
ANY IMPLIED WARRANTIES ARE LIMITED IN DURATION TO THE EXPRESS
WARRANTY PERIOD SET FORTH ABOVE.
```

**Why ALL CAPS:**
- UCC § 2-316 requires warranty disclaimers to be "conspicuous"
- ALL CAPS is the standard method to satisfy this requirement
- Failure to use conspicuous formatting may invalidate the disclaimer

## Hardware/Equipment Warranties

### Example 7 - Equipment Warranty with Exclusions
```
WARRANTY

Vendor warrants that the Equipment will be free from defects in
materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of [one year]
from delivery ("Warranty Period").

EXCLUSIONS

This warranty does not cover defects or damage caused by:
(a) misuse, abuse, negligence, or accident;
(b) modification, alteration, or repair by anyone other than Vendor;
(c) operation outside the environmental specifications;
(d) failure to follow Vendor's installation or maintenance instructions;
(e) use with non-approved components or accessories;
(f) normal wear and tear;
(g) acts of God, including but not limited to lightning, floods,
    earthquakes, or power surges.

WARRANTY SERVICE

To obtain warranty service, Customer must: (a) contact Vendor's
support within the Warranty Period; (b) provide proof of purchase;
(c) ship the Equipment to Vendor's service center, prepaid.

WARRANTY REMEDY

Vendor will, at its option, repair or replace defective Equipment
or refund the purchase price. Repaired or replacement Equipment
will be warranted for the remainder of the original Warranty Period
or [90] days, whichever is longer.
```

## Pass-Through Warranties

### Example 8 - Third-Party Product Warranty
```
THIRD-PARTY PRODUCTS

Ancillary equipment not manufactured by Vendor, such as third-party
computers, may be excluded from any warranty coverage at Vendor's
discretion.

Vendor will pass on to Customer any manufacturer's warranty of any
such ancillary equipment, to the extent permitted by the manufacturer.

VENDOR MAKES NO WARRANTY WITH RESPECT TO THIRD-PARTY PRODUCTS AND
DISCLAIMS ALL LIABILITY ARISING FROM SUCH PRODUCTS. CUSTOMER'S SOLE
REMEDY FOR DEFECTS IN THIRD-PARTY PRODUCTS IS AGAINST THE THIRD-PARTY
MANUFACTURER.
```

## Warranty of Authority/Title

### Example 9 - Authority and IP Warranties
```
Vendor represents and warrants that:

(a) AUTHORITY. Vendor has full corporate power and authority to
    enter into this Agreement and to perform its obligations hereunder;

(b) NO CONFLICT. The execution and performance of this Agreement
    will not violate any agreement to which Vendor is a party;

(c) TITLE. Vendor has good and marketable title to the Products,
    free and clear of all liens and encumbrances;

(d) ORIGINAL WORK. The Deliverables are original works of Vendor
    and do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any
    third party;

(e) NO ENCUMBRANCES. The rights granted to Customer are not subject
    to any restrictions that would prevent Customer from fully
    enjoying the benefits contemplated herein.
```

## Compliance Warranty

### Example 10 - Regulatory Compliance**
```
COMPLIANCE

Vendor represents and warrants that the Services and Deliverables
shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws,
rules, and regulations.

DISCLAIMER OF REGULATORY WARRANTY

Vendor makes no representation whatsoever that services under this
Agreement satisfy or will satisfy any requirements of any governmental
body or other organization, including, but not limited to, any
requirement of the United States Food and Drug Administration or the
International Organization for Standardization.

Customer agrees that it is Customer's responsibility to ensure that
such services are adequate to meet its regulation/certification
requirements and that all requirements of any governmental body are
Customer's responsibility.
```

## Summary: Warranty Elements by Position

| Element | Vendor Position | Customer Position |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Standard | "Industry standard" | "Highest industry standards" |
| Duration | 30-90 days | 1+ year |
| Scope | Substantial conformance | Full conformance |
| Remedy | Re-perform OR refund (vendor choice) | Re-perform, repair, replace, OR refund (customer choice) |
| Exclusivity | "Sole and exclusive remedy" | "Without prejudice to other remedies" |
| Disclaimers | Comprehensive, ALL CAPS | Limited or absent |
| Claim Window | Short (30 days) | Reasonable (upon discovery) |
