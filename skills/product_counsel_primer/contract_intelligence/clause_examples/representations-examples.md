# Representations and Warranties Clause Examples

## Authority and Organization

**Example 1 - Standard Authority Reps**
```
REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Each party represents and warrants to the other that:

(a) ORGANIZATION. It is duly organized, validly existing, and in good
    standing under the laws of its jurisdiction of organization;

(b) AUTHORITY. It has full power and authority to execute, deliver,
    and perform this Agreement;

(c) AUTHORIZATION. The execution, delivery, and performance of this
    Agreement have been duly authorized by all necessary corporate
    action;

(d) BINDING EFFECT. This Agreement constitutes a legal, valid, and
    binding obligation enforceable in accordance with its terms;

(e) NO CONFLICT. The execution and performance of this Agreement will
    not violate any law, regulation, order, or agreement to which it
    is a party or by which it is bound.
```

## Vendor/Provider Representations

**Example 2 - Comprehensive Vendor Reps**
```
VENDOR REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Vendor represents and warrants to Customer that:

(a) SERVICES. Services shall be performed in a professional and
    workmanlike manner by qualified personnel in accordance with
    industry standards;

(b) NON-INFRINGEMENT. The Services and Deliverables, as delivered,
    do not and will not infringe any third-party intellectual
    property rights;

(c) COMPLIANCE. Vendor shall comply with all applicable laws,
    regulations, and industry standards in performing the Services;

(d) NO VIRUSES. Deliverables shall be free of viruses, malware,
    disabling code, or other harmful components;

(e) PERSONNEL. Vendor personnel have the skills, training, and
    qualifications necessary to perform the Services;

(f) NO DEBARMENT. Vendor is not debarred, suspended, or excluded
    from government programs and has not been convicted of any
    offense requiring debarment;

(g) PERMITS. Vendor has all licenses, permits, and authorizations
    required to perform the Services.
```

## IP Representations

**Example 3 - IP Ownership and Rights**
```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY REPRESENTATIONS

Vendor represents and warrants that:

(a) OWNERSHIP. Vendor owns or has valid licenses to all intellectual
    property necessary to provide the Services and Deliverables;

(b) ORIGINAL WORK. Deliverables are original works of Vendor and do
    not incorporate any third-party materials except as disclosed in
    Exhibit [X] or licensed under open source terms disclosed to
    Customer;

(c) RIGHT TO LICENSE. Vendor has the right to grant the licenses
    contained in this Agreement, free from any liens, encumbrances,
    or restrictions;

(d) NO ENCUMBRANCES. The rights granted to Customer are not subject
    to any agreements with third parties that would restrict Customer's
    use as contemplated herein;

(e) EMPLOYEE AGREEMENTS. Vendor's employees and contractors have
    executed agreements assigning to Vendor all rights in work product.
```

## Data and Privacy Representations

**Example 4 - Data Protection Reps**
```
DATA PROTECTION REPRESENTATIONS

Vendor represents and warrants that:

(a) SECURITY. Vendor maintains appropriate technical and organizational
    security measures consistent with industry standards and applicable
    law;

(b) PRIVACY COMPLIANCE. Vendor complies with all applicable data
    protection and privacy laws, including GDPR and CCPA where
    applicable;

(c) CERTIFICATIONS. Vendor maintains the certifications listed in
    Exhibit [X] (e.g., SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001);

(d) BACKGROUND CHECKS. Vendor conducts appropriate background checks
    on personnel who will have access to Customer Data;

(e) NO DATA BREACHES. To Vendor's knowledge, Vendor has not experienced
    a data breach affecting the types of data it will process under
    this Agreement in the past [24] months;

(f) SUB-PROCESSORS. All sub-processors are bound by data protection
    obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement.
```

## Customer Representations

**Example 5 - Customer Reps (Vendor-Protective)**
```
CUSTOMER REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Customer represents and warrants that:

(a) AUTHORITY. Customer has full power and authority to enter into
    this Agreement and to grant the rights and licenses herein;

(b) CONTENT OWNERSHIP. Customer owns or has the right to use all
    Customer Data and content provided to Vendor;

(c) NO INFRINGEMENT. Customer Data does not infringe any third-party
    intellectual property rights or violate any applicable law;

(d) COMPLIANCE. Customer's use of the Services shall comply with all
    applicable laws and regulations;

(e) COOPERATION. Customer shall provide accurate information and
    timely cooperation as reasonably required for Vendor to perform;

(f) SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS. Customer's systems meet the minimum
    requirements specified in the Documentation.
```

## Qualified Representations

**Example 6 - Knowledge and Materiality Qualifiers**
```
QUALIFIED REPRESENTATIONS

The following qualifiers apply to the representations in this Section:

(a) KNOWLEDGE QUALIFIER. Where a representation is qualified by
    "to Vendor's knowledge" or similar language, it means the actual
    knowledge of the individuals listed in Exhibit [X], after
    reasonable inquiry.

(b) MATERIALITY. References to "material" or "materially" mean an
    impact on the value, use, or benefit that would reasonably be
    expected to influence a party's decision.

(c) MATERIAL ADVERSE EFFECT. "Material Adverse Effect" means any
    event, circumstance, or change that has or would reasonably be
    expected to have a material adverse effect on the business,
    operations, assets, or financial condition of the affected party.
```

## Anti-Corruption and Export

**Example 7 - Compliance Reps**
```
COMPLIANCE REPRESENTATIONS

Each party represents and warrants that:

(a) ANTI-CORRUPTION. Neither it nor any of its officers, directors,
    employees, or agents has made or will make any payment, gift, or
    transfer of anything of value to any government official, political
    party, or candidate for political office for the purpose of
    influencing any governmental action in connection with this
    Agreement, in violation of the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act,
    UK Bribery Act, or similar laws;

(b) EXPORT COMPLIANCE. It shall comply with all applicable export
    control laws and regulations, including the U.S. Export
    Administration Regulations and sanctions administered by OFAC;

(c) NO SANCTIONS. It is not: (i) located in, organized under the laws
    of, or ordinarily resident in a sanctioned country; (ii) a
    Specially Designated National or on any other sanctions list;

(d) ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING. It shall comply with all applicable
    anti-money laundering laws and regulations.
```

## Survival and Bring-Down

**Example 8 - Rep Survival and Repetition**
```
SURVIVAL OF REPRESENTATIONS

(a) REPETITION. All representations and warranties made as of the
    Effective Date shall be deemed repeated as of each anniversary
    and at Closing (if applicable).

(b) SURVIVAL. The representations and warranties shall survive
    expiration or termination of this Agreement for a period of
    [18/24/36] months.

(c) FUNDAMENTAL REPS. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the representations
    regarding authority, organization, and IP ownership ("Fundamental
    Representations") shall survive for the applicable statute of
    limitations plus [60] days.

(d) RELIANCE. Each party acknowledges that the other party has entered
    into this Agreement in reliance on the representations and warranties
    set forth herein.
```

## Disclosure Schedules

**Example 9 - Schedule Exceptions**
```
DISCLOSURE SCHEDULE

The representations and warranties are subject to the exceptions set
forth in the Disclosure Schedule attached hereto.

Matters disclosed in any section of the Disclosure Schedule shall be
deemed disclosed for all sections to which such disclosure is reasonably
apparent on its face.

No disclosure shall be deemed to constitute an admission that such
disclosed matter is material or would have a Material Adverse Effect.
```

## No Other Representations

**Example 10 - Disclaimer of Other Reps**
```
NO OTHER REPRESENTATIONS

EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS AGREEMENT, NEITHER PARTY MAKES
ANY REPRESENTATIONS OR WARRANTIES OF ANY KIND, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED,
STATUTORY, OR OTHERWISE.

EACH PARTY DISCLAIMS ALL IMPLIED WARRANTIES, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF
MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

Each party acknowledges that it has not relied on any representation
or warranty not expressly set forth in this Agreement.
```
