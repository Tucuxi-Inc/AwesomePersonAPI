# Settlement and Release Agreement - Expected Clauses

## Overview

Settlement agreements resolve disputes by exchanging releases for consideration, avoiding or concluding litigation. They must be carefully drafted to ensure complete resolution, prevent future claims, and address confidentiality. This skill documents expected structure and provisions based on analysis of settlement agreement samples.

## Agreement Types

| Type | Context | Typical Provisions |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| **Litigation Settlement** | Active lawsuit | Dismissal, release, payment |
| **Pre-Suit Settlement** | Threatened claim | Broad release, confidentiality |
| **Employment Settlement** | Termination/discrimination | Severance, release, non-disparagement |
| **Commercial Dispute** | Contract/business dispute | Payment, release, ongoing relationship |
| **IP Settlement** | Patent/trademark dispute | License, release, coexistence |

## Standard Structure

### Typical Section Order

1. Recitals (Background)
2. Definitions
3. Settlement Payment
4. Release of Claims
5. Dismissal (if litigation pending)
6. Confidentiality
7. Non-Disparagement
8. Representations and Warranties
9. No Admission
10. Tax Provisions
11. Enforcement/Breach
12. General Provisions

## Required Clauses

### 1. Recitals

**Must Include**:
- [ ] Identification of dispute
- [ ] Background facts
- [ ] Reference to claims/litigation
- [ ] Desire to settle

**Example**:
```
RECITALS

WHEREAS, a dispute has arisen between the parties relating to [brief
description of dispute] (the "Dispute");

WHEREAS, [if litigation pending] [Party A] filed a lawsuit against
[Party B] in [Court], Case No. [X] (the "Litigation"), alleging
claims for [breach of contract / patent infringement / etc.];

WHEREAS, the parties deny any liability and enter into this Agreement
solely to avoid the expense and uncertainty of litigation;

WHEREAS, the parties desire to fully and finally settle and resolve
the Dispute and all claims that were or could have been asserted;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises herein, the
parties agree as follows:
```

### 2. Settlement Payment

**Must Include**:
- [ ] Amount
- [ ] Payment timing
- [ ] Payment method
- [ ] Tax treatment

**Example**:
```
SETTLEMENT PAYMENT
Within [30] days of the Effective Date, [Paying Party] shall pay to
[Receiving Party] the sum of [AMOUNT] (the "Settlement Payment") by
wire transfer to the account designated by [Receiving Party].

[If structured payment:]
The Settlement Payment shall be made in [X] installments as follows:
(a) $[X] within 30 days of the Effective Date;
(b) $[X] within 90 days of the Effective Date;
(c) $[X] within 180 days of the Effective Date.

Failure to make any payment when due shall constitute a material
breach, and the entire remaining balance shall become immediately
due and payable, plus interest at [X%] per annum.
```

### 3. Releases

**General Release (Broad)**:
```
GENERAL RELEASE

[Releasing Party], for itself and its heirs, executors, administrators,
successors, and assigns, hereby fully, finally, and forever releases
and discharges [Released Party] and its parents, subsidiaries,
affiliates, officers, directors, employees, agents, attorneys,
insurers, successors, and assigns (collectively, "Releasees") from
any and all claims, demands, damages, actions, causes of action,
suits, debts, liens, contracts, agreements, liabilities, and
obligations of every kind and nature, whether known or unknown,
suspected or unsuspected, disclosed or undisclosed, that exist as
of the Effective Date, arising out of or relating to [the Dispute /
the Litigation / any matter whatsoever from the beginning of time
to the Effective Date].
```

**Mutual Release**:
```
MUTUAL RELEASE
Each party, on behalf of itself and its Related Parties, hereby
releases and forever discharges the other party and its Related
Parties from all Claims arising out of or relating to [scope].
```

**Limited Release (Narrow)**:
```
LIMITED RELEASE
[Releasing Party] releases [Released Party] solely from Claims
arising out of or relating to [specific matter/contract/incident].
This release does not extend to [carve-outs].
```

**California Civil Code Section 1542 Waiver** (IMPORTANT):
```
UNKNOWN CLAIMS WAIVER
[Releasing Party] acknowledges that it may hereafter discover facts
different from or in addition to those it now knows or believes to
be true. [Releasing Party] expressly waives and relinquishes, to
the fullest extent permitted by law, the provisions, rights, and
benefits of California Civil Code Section 1542, which provides:

"A GENERAL RELEASE DOES NOT EXTEND TO CLAIMS THAT THE CREDITOR OR
RELEASING PARTY DOES NOT KNOW OR SUSPECT TO EXIST IN HIS OR HER
FAVOR AT THE TIME OF EXECUTING THE RELEASE AND THAT, IF KNOWN BY
HIM OR HER, WOULD HAVE MATERIALLY AFFECTED HIS OR HER SETTLEMENT
WITH THE DEBTOR OR RELEASED PARTY."

[Releasing Party] waives any similar provisions under the laws of
any other state or jurisdiction.
```

### 4. Dismissal of Litigation

```
DISMISSAL
Within [5] business days of receipt of the Settlement Payment,
[Plaintiff] shall file a stipulation of dismissal with prejudice
in the form attached as Exhibit [X], with each party to bear its
own costs and attorneys' fees.

[Alternative: Dismissal without prejudice with enforcement provision]
The parties shall file a stipulation to stay the Litigation pending
performance. Upon full performance, [Plaintiff] shall dismiss with
prejudice. If [Defendant] breaches, [Plaintiff] may enforce this
Agreement or pursue the Litigation.
```

### 5. Confidentiality

**Standard Confidentiality**:
```
CONFIDENTIALITY
The parties agree to keep the terms of this Agreement strictly
confidential and shall not disclose the terms to any third party,
except: (a) to their attorneys, accountants, and financial advisors
bound by professional confidentiality; (b) to immediate family
members who agree to maintain confidentiality; (c) as required by
law or legal process; (d) as necessary to enforce this Agreement.

In the event of required disclosure, the disclosing party shall
provide advance notice to the other party where legally permitted
and shall seek confidential treatment where available.
```

**Public Statement**:
```
PUBLIC STATEMENT
If asked about the Dispute, the parties shall respond only that
"the matter has been resolved to the mutual satisfaction of the
parties" and shall make no further comment.
```

### 6. Non-Disparagement

```
NON-DISPARAGEMENT
Each party agrees not to make, publish, or communicate any
disparaging, defamatory, or derogatory statements about the other
party or its officers, directors, employees, products, or services
to any third party.

This provision shall not prohibit: (a) truthful statements required
by law; (b) statements made in legal proceedings to enforce this
Agreement; (c) internal communications among employees with a
legitimate need to know.
```

## Common Clauses

### 7. No Admission of Liability

```
NO ADMISSION
This Agreement is entered into solely for the purpose of avoiding
the expense and uncertainty of litigation. Nothing herein shall be
construed as an admission of liability, wrongdoing, or fault by any
party. Each party expressly denies any and all liability.
```

### 8. Return of Property/Materials

```
RETURN OF PROPERTY
Within [15] days of the Effective Date, [Party] shall return to
[Other Party] all property, documents, and materials belonging to
[Other Party], including [specific items]. [Party] shall certify
in writing that all such materials have been returned or destroyed.
```

### 9. Covenant Not to Sue

```
COVENANT NOT TO SUE
Each party covenants and agrees that it will not, directly or
indirectly, commence, maintain, or prosecute any action or proceeding
against the other party arising out of or relating to the released
Claims. Any breach of this covenant shall entitle the other party
to recover all damages, costs, and attorneys' fees incurred as a
result of the breach.
```

### 10. Representations and Warranties

```
REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
Each party represents and warrants that:

(a) Authority. It has full power and authority to execute this
    Agreement and to grant the releases herein.

(b) No Assignment. It has not assigned or transferred any Claims
    released herein to any third party.

(c) No Other Claims. It is not aware of any Claims against the
    other party other than those released herein.

(d) Legal Advice. It has had the opportunity to consult with legal
    counsel of its choosing and has entered into this Agreement
    voluntarily and with full knowledge of its terms.
```

### 11. Tax Provisions

```
TAX ALLOCATION
The Settlement Payment shall be allocated as follows for tax purposes:
(a) $[X] as [wages / damages / other]; (b) $[X] as [category].

Each party shall be responsible for its own taxes arising from this
Agreement. [Receiving Party] agrees to indemnify [Paying Party] for
any taxes, penalties, or interest arising from [Receiving Party's]
failure to report the Settlement Payment.

[If employment settlement:]
[Employer] shall withhold applicable taxes from the portion allocated
to wages and shall issue IRS Form W-2. The remainder shall be reported
on IRS Form 1099.
```

### 12. Enforcement

```
ENFORCEMENT
In any action to enforce this Agreement, the prevailing party shall
be entitled to recover its reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.

[With retention of jurisdiction:]
The [Court] shall retain jurisdiction to enforce the terms of this
Agreement. The parties consent to the Court's jurisdiction for
enforcement purposes.
```

## Optional Clauses (Context-Specific)

### 13. Employment-Specific Provisions

```
ADEA/OWBPA COMPLIANCE
[Employee] acknowledges: (a) this Agreement is written in a manner
calculated to be understood; (b) this Agreement specifically refers
to rights under the Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA);
(c) [Employee] does not waive rights arising after the date of this
Agreement; (d) [Employee] has been advised in writing to consult
with an attorney; (e) [Employee] has [21/45] days to consider this
Agreement; (f) [Employee] has [7] days following execution to revoke.

This Agreement shall not become effective until the revocation
period has expired without revocation.
```

### 14. Cooperation

```
COOPERATION
The parties agree to cooperate in good faith to effectuate the terms
of this Agreement, including executing any additional documents and
taking any actions reasonably necessary to implement the settlement.
```

### 15. Non-Solicitation of Employees

```
NON-SOLICITATION
For [12] months following the Effective Date, neither party shall
directly solicit for employment any employee of the other party who
was involved in the Dispute or the negotiation of this Agreement.
```

### 16. Injunctive Relief

```
INJUNCTIVE RELIEF
The parties acknowledge that a breach of the confidentiality or
non-disparagement provisions would cause irreparable harm not
adequately compensated by money damages. The non-breaching party
shall be entitled to injunctive relief without posting bond.
```

## Red Flags - Missing Elements

| Missing Element | Risk Level | Implication |
|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| No Section 1542 waiver | HIGH | Unknown claims survive |
| Vague release scope | HIGH | Future claims possible |
| No confidentiality | MEDIUM | Terms become public |
| Missing dismissal mechanics | HIGH | Litigation continues |
| No authority rep | MEDIUM | Agreement voidable |
| No breach remedy | MEDIUM | Enforcement difficult |
| Missing tax allocation | MEDIUM | Tax disputes |
| No ADEA compliance | HIGH | Release unenforceable (employment) |

## Key Decision Points

1. **Release Scope**: General vs. limited release?
2. **Mutual vs. One-Way**: Both parties releasing?
3. **Payment Structure**: Lump sum or installments?
4. **Confidentiality**: Public or confidential settlement?
5. **Dismissal**: With or without prejudice?
6. **Unknown Claims**: Section 1542 waiver?
7. **Non-Disparagement**: Mutual or one-way?
8. **Enforcement**: Retained jurisdiction?
9. **Tax Treatment**: How to allocate payment?
10. **Timing**: Review period required (ADEA)?

## Sample Provisions

### Comprehensive Mutual Release
```
Each party releases the other from all claims, known or unknown,
arising from or relating to [scope]. Each party waives California
Civil Code Section 1542 and similar provisions. The releases shall
be binding on successors and assigns.
```

### Employment Settlement Core Terms
```
In consideration of [severance], Employee releases Employer from all
claims arising from employment and separation, including under Title VII,
ADEA, ADA, FMLA, and state law equivalents. Employee has [21] days to
consider and [7] days to revoke. Employer shall provide neutral
reference. Both parties agree to non-disparagement.
```

### Commercial Dispute Settlement
```
[Defendant] shall pay [Plaintiff] $[X] within 30 days. Upon payment,
Plaintiff shall dismiss the Litigation with prejudice. Each party
releases the other from all claims relating to the [Contract/Dispute].
Terms are confidential. Each party bears its own fees and costs.
```
